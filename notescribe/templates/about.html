Welcome to Notescribe

Who are we?

We are an open source project that aims to make music transcription easy.

How does this work?

Notescribe takes your music and runs it through a machine learning model called a transformer, written in pytorch. Our transformer will convert your music into the midi file format. We then take the midi, and convert it to sheet music using lilypond. Then, we upload the sheet music to AWS s3 to store, and give you the files to enjoy.
